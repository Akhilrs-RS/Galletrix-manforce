# ManForce ERP: Executive Overview

## 1. Vision
ManForce ERP is a centralized, digital ecosystem designed to streamline workforce management, compliance tracking, and client relationship management for industrial and construction sectors. It replaces fragmented, paper-based workflows with a unified, data-driven platform.

## 2. Key Capabilities
ManForce ERP empowers stakeholders with clear operational visibility through four integrated modules:

### A. Workforce & Compliance Management
- **Real-time Deployment**: Track worker status (Available, Deployed, On Leave) and optimize site-specific allocation.
- **Automated Compliance**: Centralized storage for visas, labor cards, and IDs with proactive alerts for expiring documents.
- **Attendance & Payroll**: Automated tracking of daily check-ins, overtime hours, and net salary calculations.

### B. Operations & Financials
- **Invoice & Client Tracking**: Monitor revenue per client, track payment statuses, and manage contracts.
- **Recruitment Pipeline**: A streamlined hiring funnel (from application to hiring) to build a robust workforce.

### C. Client Relationship Management (CRM)
- **Kanban Pipeline**: Visualize and manage sales deals through stages (Lead, Contacted, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost).
- **Contact Lifecycle**: Manage client interactions and relationship history in one unified view.

## 3. Business Value
- **Operational Efficiency**: Drastically reduces manual data entry and human error by digitizing records and automating routine calculations (payroll/attendance).
- **Compliance Risk Mitigation**: Prevents legal and financial penalties through automated expiry alerts for critical worker documents.
- **Data-Driven Decision Making**: Provides executives and managers with real-time analytics on labor utilization, revenue growth, and recruitment performance.
- **Scalability**: Designed as a modern, cloud-ready architecture that can grow alongside the business.

## 4. Technology & Security
- **Secure Access**: Robust, role-based access control ensures that sensitive worker and financial data is only accessible to authorized personnel.
- **Reliable Infrastructure**: Built on enterprise-standard technologies (PostgreSQL, Node.js) ensuring high data integrity and long-term system stability.
- **Professional Design**: An intuitive interface minimizes training time, ensuring rapid team adoption and immediate productivity gains.
